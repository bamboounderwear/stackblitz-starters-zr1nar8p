Innovation Roadmap: Building a Competitive Moat

Our vision extends beyond the MVP. We have a pragmatic plan to integrate cutting-edge features, building a long-term defensible advantage by solving core operational challenges.

Phase 1: Crawl (Year 1)

Focus: AI-Powered Automation & Efficiency

  • AI Fan Support Chatbot: Handle common fan inquiries 24/7 to free up staff time.
  • Automated Fan Segmentation: Use basic ML to automatically tag fans (e.g., "At-Risk of Churn," "High-Value Spender") for easier targeted marketing.

Phase 2: Walk (Years 2-3)

Focus: Revenue Optimization & Enhanced Experience

  • Predictive Ticketing Analytics: Forecast attendance for upcoming games to optimize promotions and staffing.
  • Personalized Recommendations: AI engine to surface the most relevant content and merchandise to each fan.
  • Augmented Reality (AR) Experiences: In-stadium AR scavenger hunts and photo filters to create buzz and social sharing.

Phase 3: Run (Years 4+)

Focus: Generative AI & Frictionless Loyalty

  • Generative AI for Content: Automatically create post-game summaries and personalized fan messages.
  • Card-Linked Offers: Allow fans to automatically earn points by linking their credit card and shopping at team sponsors, extending loyalty beyond the stadium.
  • Hyper-Personalized Fan Journeys: Use AI to guide each fan along an automated, individualized path that maximizes their lifetime value.
